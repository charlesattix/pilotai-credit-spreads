Sharpe Ceiling Analysis (RES-003 + output/sharpe_ceiling_analysis.md)
The math: For binary-outcome trades (win prob p, win +w, loss −l):
SR_annual = SR_trade × √N_annual
At 85% win rate, +19% avg win, −47% avg loss, 208 trades/yr:
Theoretical max Sharpe = 0.386 × √208 = 5.57
Key finding (RES-003): Renaissance Medallion full-period net Sharpe is ~2.0–2.5. The 6.0+ figures are gross-of-fees, cherry-picked sub-periods. Honest ceiling for our scale: 2.5–5.6 depending on trade count and win rate. We are at 4.10–4.58 — well positioned.

Failed Experiments — What We Learned
ExperimentVerdictRoot CauseLesson
EXP-400 ML Filter FAIL AUC ~0.0 (near chance). 2024 OOS: −39%. Wrong base config. EXP-400 has 2024 regime mismatch. ML can't fix a bad underlying strategy.
Tactical Regime Sizing DNE 1.5× fires 70% of days (signals agree). In 2022 it fired only 6% — amplified the bear. Consensus sizing assumes consensus = quality. In trending markets, all signals agree AND are right. In 2022, all signals agreed late, amplifying mean-reversion losses.
Safe Kelly NEAR-MISS 98% of trades skipped across 6 years. Circuit breaker fires on compound equity peak. Annual DD reset needed. Compound equity peak causes brakes to fire in good years after one bad year (2020 peak, 2021 recovery triggers brakes at lower threshold).
Event Vol Machine (FOMC/CPI) FAIL Avg P&L/share FOMC: −$0.16. CPI: +$0.10. Net −0.2%/yr over 94 trades. IV crush happens before T+1 close: options are priced more accurately at event time, not the following day. Need intraday T+0 exit 30 min after announcement.
CBOE SKEW Overlay DNE 1.25× boost in complacent months amplified losses. MaxDD −34%→−43%. Combo regime already filters worst SKEW conditions. SKEW overlay double-counts the risk signal already embedded in VIX/regime detector.
1DTE Bull Put Spread FAIL 18 trades / 4 years. +0.6%/yr avg. Black-Scholes pricing only (real prices $0.01 tick). Regime filter blocks most 1DTE entries. Need real 1DTE bid/ask data. Viable after data backfill for true BULL days with proper credits.
Short DTE (3–15 days) FAIL DTE=15 best (+7.9% avg) but −94.9% DD. All others negative avg return. Short DTE at 35-DTE regime filter means too few entries per year. Utilization 22–68%. 35DTE is the correct DTE for this combo regime framework.
