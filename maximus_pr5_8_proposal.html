📋 Executive Summary

Our original rebuild plan had 5 phases focused on rebuilding the paper trader from scratch. After reviewing Charles's production architecture, 3 of our 5 phases are largely redundant with services that already exist and are battle-tested. Instead of rebuilding, we should integrate with the existing architecture and focus our effort on the pieces that are genuinely missing.

Bottom line: We should skip rebuilding what Charles already built, wire our modules into his pipeline, and focus on the ZeroDTE strategy integration and paper trading mode within the existing ExecutionEngine.

  • Pending order reconciliation

PARTIALLY REDUNDANT.

REDUNDANT. Charles's shared/reconciler.py already handles:
  • pending_open → open (fill confirmed)
  • pending_open → failed_open (terminal/404)

  • Reconciliation event logging
This is exactly what our Phase 4 was going to build. Skip entirely.

❓ Open Questions for Charles

  1. Paper trading mode: Does ExecutionEngine's dry_run mode write to the DB (simulating fills)? Or does it skip DB writes entirely? For paper trading we need simulated fills recorded in the DB so PositionMonitor can manage them.
  2. Per-strategy exit thresholds: PositionMonitor uses global MANAGEMENT_DTE_THRESHOLD = 21. For 0DTE, we need ZERO_DTE_MANAGEMENT_DTE_THRESHOLD = 0. Does PositionMonitor support per-strategy overrides, or do we need to add that?
  3. 0DTE timing: PositionMonitor runs every 5 minutes. For 0DTE positions that expire same-day, is 5-minute granularity sufficient? Or do we need a faster loop for 0DTE specifically?
  4. Live pricing integration: How does PositionMonitor currently get option prices for P&L checks? Alpaca last_price? Polygon snapshots? Our shared/live_pricing.py uses Polygon — should we wire it in as the price source?
  5. VIX regime wiring: Where exactly should shared/vix_regime.py plug in? Into AlertPositionSizer (Stage 4)? Or into RiskGate as an additional check?
  6. Assignment/pin risk for live: Our backtester handles deep ITM shorts ≤2 DTE. Does PositionMonitor have equivalent logic for live positions?
